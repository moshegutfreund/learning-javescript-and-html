Notable Accomplishments in Machine Learning
Person / Company Date Short Description
Fei-Fei Li 2007 Fei-Fei Li Fei-Fei Li is an American computer scientist and the Sequoia Capital Professor of Computer Science at Stanford University. She created ImageNet which is a massive database of nearly 15 million images categorized into 22,000 different categories which is used to train machines into recognizing different objects that they see.
Google Brain Team 2012 Researchers led by Adrew Ng and Jeff Dean, developed a neural netqork that was able to learn how to identify different cats from watching YouTube videos, one at a time.
DeepFace, by Facebook 2014 Researchers at Facebook create a system of neural networks that use machine learning to identify face with over 95% accuracy.
AlphaGo, by Google 2016 Google became the first computer that can beat an unhaddicapped professional human player in the game. It used a combination of learning and tree search techniques, which was adapted to Chess in 2017.
Infervision 2021 Infervision an AI tool used for medical imaging solutions. It was originally designed to be able to read CT scans and detect lung cancer but has since been adapted to detect Covid infections. By automating this process healthcare professionals can expand access to care and improve it for everyone.
